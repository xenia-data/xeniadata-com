2. What Xenia is

Xenia is a hotel data aggregator. Xenia collects, organizes, validates, and displays factual information about hotel and lodging properties. Xenia produces three kinds of information:

  1. Verified facts (Tier 1). Objective attributes cross-validated across multiple lawful sources or licensed feeds.
  2. Self-reported attributes (Tier 2). Claims made by property owners about their own properties, attributed to the property.

7.2 Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3 distinction

When you access information on the Service, you will see one of three classifications:

  • Verified Fact (Tier 1). Cross-validated; displayed without attribution as a factual statement.
  • Self-Reported (Tier 2). The property's own claim; displayed with explicit attribution to the property.
  • Xenia Insight (Tier 3). Derived inference; displayed with methodology disclosure and a confidence score.

Always check the tier label before relying on a specific data point.

If you require absolute confirmation of a sensitive attribute (e.g., wheelchair accessibility, kosher kitchen) for booking purposes, contact the property directly. Xenia attestations are informational, not warranties.
