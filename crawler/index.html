Crawler conduct

What the Xenia crawler does, what it doesn't, and how to reach us.

User agent

XeniaCrawler/1.0 (+https://xeniadata.com/crawler/)

Conduct rules

  • Strictly respects robots.txt directives for our user agent
  • Honors Crawl-delay hints
  • Limits concurrent requests per origin
  • Identifies itself in every request via the user-agent string above
  • Does not attempt to bypass anti-bot or rate-limiting measures
  • Does not crawl behind login walls or paywalls
  • Does not download or process media files beyond what is needed to verify their presence

What we collect

The Xenia crawler retrieves publicly available factual information from hotel websites and a small registry of public reference sources. We extract structured facts (amenities, location, contact information, room counts, etc.) for inclusion in our hospitality data product.

What we don't collect

  • Personally identifiable information about guests or staff
  • Payment data or reservation systems
  • Login-gated or paywalled content
  • Verbatim text from gated third-party platforms

How to block the crawler

Add the following to your robots.txt to fully exclude Xenia:

User-agent: XeniaCrawler
Disallow: /

How to remove your property from our index

If you operate a hotel and want to remove your property from the Xenia dataset, contact hotels@xeniadata.com. We will verify authority and process your request within 30 days.

Crawler issues

Crawler issues, abuse reports, or technical concerns: crawler@xeniadata.com
