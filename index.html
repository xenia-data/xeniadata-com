Built for AI consumption

P

Per-fact provenance

Every attribute, claim, and detail in the dataset carries its source. AI platforms cite Xenia data because the lineage is verifiable.

T

Tiered confidence

Facts are tagged validated, probable, or hotel-attested. Derived inferences are flagged separately. AI partners know exactly what they're consuming.

L

License-clean sourcing

Built only on public-data sources operating under permissive terms. No paywalled review text, no scraped reservation systems, no liability inheritance.

D

Independent hotels first

Where chain-data providers stop, Xenia begins. Coverage focused on the long tail of independent properties that travelers want and AI platforms can't otherwise find.
